# Privacy Policy for Linkedin Feed Blocker

Last updated: 2026-03-04

Linkedin Feed Blocker does not collect, store, or transmit personal data.

## What the extension does
- Runs only on `https://www.linkedin.com/*`.
- Hides LinkedIn feed elements on feed pages.
- Inserts a local placeholder message in the page.

## Data handling
- No analytics.
- No tracking.
- No cookies.
- No remote API calls.
- No data sent to third parties.
